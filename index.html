Elijah Guptill

Hello, my name is Elijah Guptill and I am a blended Bachelors and Masters in mathematics student at California Polytechnic University. I have varied interests in mathematics, including, functional analysis, point-set topology, topological data analysis, and homology.

I have been funded by the Frost Fund within the California Polytechnic University Bailey College of Science and Mathematics to research the complex dimensions of modified Sierpinski carpets, moduli spaces of triangles, and the topology of sequences generated by the evaluation of function sequences, with, Sean Watson, Eric Brussel, and Ryan-Tully Doyle, respectively.
